How It Works

Face Recognition Analysis

Our AI scans facial expressions to detect emotions like stress, anxiety, and sadness. It tracks changes over time, providing real-time feedback and support. Users receive insights into mood patterns and personalized well-being recommendations.

Screen Test

A clinically backed test assesses symptoms of anxiety, depression, and stress. AI analyzes responses to give instant feedback and suggest self-care or professional help. The test helps users understand their mental well-being in a structured way.

Journal Entries

A guided journal allows users to record thoughts and emotions daily. AI-generated prompts encourage self-reflection and emotional tracking. Over time, journal insights help recognize patterns and improve emotional resilience.

Chatbot Support

A 24/7 AI chatbot provides emotional support, coping techniques, and mindfulness exercises. It recognizes distress signals and can escalate to a therapist if needed. Users receive instant, compassionate assistance anytime, anywhere.

Treatments and Recommendations

Based on assessments, users get personalized treatment plans and therapist recommendations. The system filters therapists by specialization and location for tailored support. It also suggests therapy sessions, self-care resources, and wellness activities.
